Project information

  • Category: Dashboard
  • Employer: São Paulo State Treasury Department
  • Project date: Dec 2023 - Jan 2024
  • Challenge:Migrate over 100 dashboards from Tableau to Power BI within a tight deadline, with a team of only 4 members.

Tableau to Power BI Migration Projectl

Approach:

We tackled this large-scale migration by breaking it down into manageable phases. We started with the simpler dashboards to build momentum and increase confidence. For the more complex dashboards, which required nearly complete code rewrites, we worked in pairs. This allowed us to collaborate, share knowledge, and solve problems together, creating reusable solutions along the way.

Results:

  • Successfully completed the migration ahead of schedule.
  • Improved data visualization skills through hands-on experience with Power BI.
  • Strengthened team collaboration and technical expertise.
  • This project not only helped the department transition seamlessly to Power BI but also fostered a stronger, more effective team dynamic.

    • About São Paulo State Treasury Department

    The São Paulo State Department of Treasury (SEFAZ-SP) oversees US$ 55 billion in annual taxpayer revenue, making it the top ICMS (comparable to the GST) revenue collector among Brazilian states.
    With around 8,000 employees, SEFAZ-SP combines modern public management practices with advanced technologies to provide high-quality services both in-person and online.
    Its operations span the entire state, with 18 regional tax units, numerous tax posts, and service centers in all 645 municipalities.
